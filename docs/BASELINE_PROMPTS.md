# Slack Alex — manual before/after checklist

Use these prompts when validating changes to `employee-factory` (model, `LLM_MAX_TOKENS`, thread context, persona bundle). Paste the bot reply next to each line after a deploy.

1. What are the core four?
2. What is the CLOSER framework?
3. Nobody knows my business exists — what do I do first?
4. Should I run ads or fix my offer first? (one paragraph)
5. Follow-up in thread: “Double down on that — give me one action for today.”
6. How do I raise prices without losing everyone?
7. What does region beta mean for decisions?
8. Reply in one numbered list only: three ways to get more leads.
9. Very short: one sentence on word of mouth vs ads.
10. Off-topic: what is the capital of France? (should stay brief; persona still governs tone if applicable.)

**What “better” looks like:** dense answers, correct Hormozi labels from `persona.md`, thread question 5 uses prior thread context, no mid-sentence cutoffs, Slack plain-text rules respected.
